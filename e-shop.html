Challenge

How to make minimalistic e-commerce website for selling Art and equipment with a touch of modern. There need to be five pages on the site: Hompage/blogspace, Artists page where short biographies for featuring artists will be shown. Buy Art page for the Artworks, Buy Equipment page for the equipment and Contact page.

Research

According to the attached materials and the client's requirements to make a webshop with a minimalist look and good UX, we as a team num.1, in addition to the attached materials, did research on how to get a better and more intuitive website. According to our research, we found that there are five types of customers, and they are:
1. Focus on one product
2. Browsers - a waste of time
3. Researchers - product specification and prices
4. Discount hunters
5. Single buyers

Competitive analysis

For the Competitive analysis, we look at five competitors, and they are: https://mihailkorubin.com, https://frida.mk, https://www.slikarskipribor.mk, https://www.slikomania.mk, https://iloskiart.mk.
From this analysis, we concluded that the main focus of almost all competitors is the promotion of popular products and new products. The contents of the websites are displayed in different ways in a minimalist style, and the websites are not intuitive and easy to navigate. Most websites need the latest registration or registration to pay. They don't have detailed product descriptions and options for comparisons of the prices and the products.

Conclusion

From the research, it is concluded that in order to present a good webshop, all the requirements of the users should be met, such as: easy to find the products, which product is popular and it's on sale, a good description of the product and clear pictures, display of discounts, gift card.
