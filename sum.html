Challenge

In the interview with Brainster, we concluded that we need to make a free platform with an audio player, which will consist of any background noise. The platform should positively affect the productivity of everyone who will use it.

The Process

To reach the final solution, we went through several processes such as Research, Competitive analysis, Persona, and interviews with users and stakeholders.

Research

According to the research we did, we found out that a large number of research has been done by scientists about the relationship between the effect of audio and the work. According to them, listening to music or any background noise while working, have a positive effect on our concentration and pleasure.

Competitive analysis

For Competitive analysis, we took three competitors, and they are:

Noisli - https://www.noisli.com/

Noisli is a website that has a large selection of sounds from which the user can choose. It has a simple minimalist design with intuitive icons that make it easy for the user to use. There is an option to save the combo that the user has selected, as well as the ability to turn on a timer for more focused operation. For more sound options there is an upgrade pro with a subscription by the user. One of Noisli's features is a distraction-free text editor, although it is still in beta. It also has the option to create playlists.

myNoise - https://mynoise.net

This website offers a large selection of audio and music for every user's mood. It has a good player system and offers many options through sliders with which you can adjust each individual sound. There is no charge for using this site, but there is a possibility of patreon, where if the user wants he can make a one-time payment as a sign of support for the creator of the site. In terms of design, there is too much information on the page and at first glance, it may be difficult for the user.

Noises - https://noises.online/

This website also offers a large selection of sounds that can be combined. It has a simple and intuitive design. The user has the option to share a link. There is no charge, and there is no registration option.

Interviews with users

From all the user's interviews, we have concluded that most listen to the audio while working. The type of audio they listen to depends on the complexity of the project they are working on and is usually without text. The most common types of audio devices are mobile and desktop computers. Because they work on a desktop computer, they often use different web platforms to listen to audio. We concluded that at work, notifications interfere with them and reduce their focus. The design is considered to be minimalist and intuitive, preferably in darker colors.

Conclusion

As a result of all the information we received, we decided that the most important thing is the design of this type of web app to be minimalist, intuitive, and easy to use. After interviews with users, we came up with a solution using darker colors that match the colors of the designers. With the information from the competitive analysis, we came to the conclusion that there should not be too much information on the page, for the main focus of the user to be on the radio player, as well as to make the user more satisfied.
